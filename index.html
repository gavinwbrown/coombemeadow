This unique cabin has a wood burner, super king or twin beds. Artic bedding, electric blankets, 4 hot water bottles and throws. A kitchenette, with a microwave, small fridge, sink, double gas camping hob with grill, kettle and toaster. Tastefully decorated with antiques. Attached is a private hot shower, composting toilet and fire pit.
Sleep with the wildlife of Dartmoor in an open sided cabin with curtains overlooking a spring fed swimming pond, and into ancient woodland frequented Devon wildlife. You will hear more horses than cars on the distant mossy lane. There's a stream, ponds, waterfalls & bridges. Your hosts live out of sight down the valley.
A 35 minute country walk from Ashburton and the famous Ashburton Cookery School; details of local walks, points of interest, pubs and restaurants are available in the cabin. The composting toilet and hot shower are three steps from the cabin in a separate tent. Unlimited wood supplied free for the wood burner and fire pits.
